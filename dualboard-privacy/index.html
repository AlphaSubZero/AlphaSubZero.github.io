Privacy Policy

App name: Dual Board: Chess & Checkers

Package name: com.chesscheckers.dualboard

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Overview

Dual Board: Chess & Checkers is an offline board game that combines chess and checkers on two linked boards. The app is designed to work without user accounts, registration, online profiles, social features, or personal data collection.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal information from users.

The app does not require users to provide a name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, files, or any other personal information.

Local App Data

The app may store some data locally on your device, such as:

  • game settings;
  • selected themes;
  • unlocked themes;
  • victory progress;
  • premium unlock status;
  • basic game preferences.

This data is stored only on your device and is used only to make the app work correctly and remember your preferences. We do not receive this data and do not transfer it to our servers.

Purchases and Premium Content

Dual Board: Chess & Checkers may offer optional Premium content through Google Play Billing. All payments and purchase processing are handled by Google Play.

We do not receive or store your payment card details, bank information, billing address, or other payment information. The app only checks whether Premium content has been purchased, so that the relevant content can be unlocked.

Advertising

Dual Board: Chess & Checkers does not currently display ads.

Analytics and Tracking

The app does not use third-party analytics tools, advertising trackers, or user profiling systems.

Internet Access

The app may use Google Play services only when needed for purchase verification and Premium content unlocking. The main gameplay works offline.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal information from any users, it also does not knowingly collect such information from children.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user personal data with third parties.

Data Security

Since the app does not collect personal information, there is no personal user database maintained by us. Local app settings and progress remain on the user's device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
