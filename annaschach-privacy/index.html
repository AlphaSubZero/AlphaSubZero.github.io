Privacy Policy for AnnaSchach

Effective date: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AnnaSchach handles information when you use the mobile application.

1. App Overview

AnnaSchach is a chess game for Android. The app provides offline chess gameplay, themed chess boards, custom chess pieces, and optional premium features.

2. Information We Collect

AnnaSchach does not require users to create an account and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, location data, photos, or other personal information directly from users.

The app may store game-related settings locally on your device, such as selected themes, premium status, selected chess engine options, saved games, and app preferences. This information is stored on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

3. In-App Purchases

AnnaSchach offers an optional Premium upgrade through Google Play Billing. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details or billing information.

The app may use Google Play Billing to check whether Premium has been purchased or restored for the Google account used on the device.

4. Advertising

AnnaSchach currently does not display third-party advertising.

5. Analytics and Tracking

AnnaSchach currently does not use third-party analytics services and does not track users across apps or websites.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. Google Play may process purchase-related information when you use Google Play Billing, according to Google's own policies.

7. Children's Privacy

AnnaSchach is not specifically directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Security

Since AnnaSchach does not operate user accounts or external servers for gameplay data, game settings and saved data are stored locally on your device. You can remove this local data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data in Android settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
