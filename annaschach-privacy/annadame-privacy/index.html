AnnaDame Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

AnnaDame is an Android checkers game developed by Roman Kušnir.

Information We Collect

AnnaDame does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or messages.

Local Data

The app may store game settings, selected themes, game progress, and Premium unlock status locally on your device. This data is not uploaded to our servers.

In-App Purchases

AnnaDame offers an optional Premium upgrade through Google Play Billing. Purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Ads

AnnaDame does not currently display ads.

Analytics and Tracking

AnnaDame does not use third-party analytics or tracking tools.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

Children

AnnaDame is a general audience checkers game. The app is not specifically directed to children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
